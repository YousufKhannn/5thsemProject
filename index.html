Computer Science Engineering

The department of computer science and Engg., at Anjuman E Islam Polytechnic, Gadag is renowned for imparting diploma education and preparing its students for real world challenges. Computer science department is started with an intake of 45 in 1992-93 and later increased to 60.To provide good quality teaching to the students, computer science department is housed in a built up area of 7000 Sq.ft. of carpet area with well furnished class rooms and laboratories. The department has state of art labs with latest hardware and softwares with internet facility.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The department has been established in the year 1984. The department is housed in a total area of 18000 Sq ft. and is well furnished. The department has been equipped with sufficient facilities to impart training and to develop technical skills and competencies of students. The department is successfully training the students for facing campus interviews conducted by various companies and get selected in the firms like Kalyani, Jindal, BHEL, BEL etc Grant in Aid status is awarded by the Government of Karnataka in the year 2001.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

Since the inception of Anjuman E Islam Polytechnic in 1983 the Electronics & Communication discipline is in force. It was started as Telecommunication discipline with a intake of 45 students. Over the years it has grown in leaps and bounds. The intake is 60 students at present. The department is spread over a carpet area of 7000 Sq.ft. with well equipped laboratories and class rooms. The labs are regularly upgraded as per the demands of the syllabus and AICTE norms. Laboratories have been modernized under the AICTE MODROBS scheme .

Mechanical Engineering

The department has been established in the year 1993. The department has been equipped with sufficient facilities to impart training and to develop technical skills and competency of students. The laboratories have been modernized under AICTE MODROB Scheme. The department is successfully training the students for facing campus interviews. Our students are placed in various companies like Toyoto, Jindal steel, Baja Autos every year through campus interviews successfully.

Civil Engineering

The department is established in the year 1983 with Civil Engg. (PHE) Course and is housed in about 15,000 Sq. ft. of floor area All the laboratories are well equipped and are modernized with the latest equipments and machines under AICTE MODROBS scheme. Department is having full fledged, dedicated and experienced staff. Students have bagged state ranks in final year during the previous years which shows the commitment of staff and students.
